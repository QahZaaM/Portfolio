ABOUT ME

See that gamer to the left? Well that's me. My name is Maaz Haq and I am in school to become a game developer. I am currently in school at triOS College for Video Game Design & Development. The reason I got into game design and deveopment was because I always thought it was such a cool thing to be able to make your own games. Another reason was because going into my final year of high school, I had no idea what I wanted to do until I took a Computer Science course and I loved it. I have been coding ever since and since all I do is play games, this fits perfectly.
