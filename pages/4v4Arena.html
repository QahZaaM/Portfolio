4v4 Arena

Blood Sport is a 4v4 arena last team standing wins the game. The objective of the game is to kill their team before they kill yours. There will be 4 classes available, Mage, Warrior, Hunter and Rogue. Each class will have an ultimate ability that requires ultimate energy which you get from dealing damage or a pick-up that spawns in the map. The only way to heal is to get the healing pick-up.


This game is currently in development
