A Pirates Duel

One ship, two pirates, and a whole lot of action. This game is developed in Unity3D and features two pirates clashing on a pirate ship. With a full pirate themed environment and sound track, you can experience what it was like to be a pirate. This game was made during PTBO Game Jam 04. Download the game right here and try it out!
